Nutrition Tips for Growing Children

Proper nutrition is essential for a child’s growth, brain development, and immune system.

Balanced Diet

Children need a balanced diet including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, proteins, and dairy products.

Healthy Eating Habits

Encourage regular meal times and reduce processed foods or sugary drinks.

Hydration

Ensure children drink enough water throughout the day to stay hydrated and support overall health.
