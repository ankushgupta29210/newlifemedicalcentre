Growth Milestones Every Parent Should Know

Children grow and develop rapidly in the first few years of life. Monitoring milestones helps parents understand whether development is progressing normally.

Physical Development

Babies usually begin rolling over around 4 months, sitting by 6 months, and walking between 9–15 months.

Speech Development

Most babies begin babbling by 6 months and may say simple words such as “mama” or “dada” around 12 months.

When to Seek Advice

If a child is significantly delayed in achieving milestones, it is important to consult a pediatrician for evaluation.
