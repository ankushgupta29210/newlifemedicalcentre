Why Vaccination is Important

Vaccination is one of the most effective ways to protect children from serious and potentially life-threatening diseases. Vaccines help the body build immunity against infections such as measles, polio, hepatitis, and pneumonia.

At our clinic, all vaccines are stored using proper cold-chain protocols to maintain their safety and effectiveness. We follow the vaccination schedule recommended by the Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP).
