What is the difference between a Pediatrician and a Neonatologist?
While a Pediatrician treats infants, children, and adolescents, a Neonatologist (which requires a D.M. super-specialty degree) is an expert specifically trained to handle the most complex and high-risk situations for newborns. Dr. Batra holds both qualifications, making her highly equipped to care for premature or ill infants.
Do you provide standard and optional vaccinations?
