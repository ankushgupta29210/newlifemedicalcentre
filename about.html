Professional Background

Dr Sakshi Batra is a Pediatrician and Neonatologist with over 9 years of experience in newborn and child healthcare.

She completed her MBBS from UCMS & GTB Hospital, MD in Pediatrics from LHMC & Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital, and DM in Neonatology from Dr Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital, New Delhi.

Her expertise includes care of high-risk and preterm newborns, neonatal intensive care, and advanced respiratory support.

She is an active member of the Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) and the National Neonatology Forum (NNF).

Areas of Expertise

  • Newborn and neonatal intensive care
  • Care of high-risk and preterm babies
  • Vaccination and immunization
  • Childhood illness management
  • Growth and development monitoring
